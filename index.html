How To Play
Left Click and Then Space to Start Playing
WASD to Move, SPACE to jump
H - Show this help menu
R - Reset the game
Left CLick - Shoot standard blue ammo
Right Click - Shoot heavier orange ammo
Move mouse to aim
Knock out all the targets before time expires!
